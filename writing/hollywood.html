hollywood boulevard

heart's racing trying to go faster so they'll stop honking
even though it's a red light, there's nowhere i can go
pushing through the headlights all so i can get to alfred coffee
and buy my seven dollar coffee as a pick me up
before their seven pm closing
rummaging through store labels
keto, paleo, ingredients invalidated
as fast as the salads are restocked
anxiety through traffic to get to work
work to pay for overpriced vegan coffee
so that i'll have the energy to restart
because it's as if once you know this lifestyle
starbucks just won't do
navigate
home resume projects writing music
