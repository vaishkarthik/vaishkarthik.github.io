no

based on know my name by chanel miller
she's taught that no means no,
that nothing means no,
why she didn't say no?
but how could she say no
when she couldn't even speak?
how could she prove her words
when she didn't see it coming?
was she supposed to document it
even when she was too frightened to fight?
or should she have seen it coming,
because she's been taught
to stay in after eight, and
to pull her shirt until they reach
the waistline of her pants
while he's been taught
that his medals weigh more than his character,
and that crimes are innocent
when they've been laced with a bit of tequila
while she's been asked
not what he did to her, not if she's okay
but where she went to get tacos,
which toppings she chose, and when she had tacos
she's probably wishing she hadn't gone to get tacos
more than she wishes she hadn't gone to that party.
