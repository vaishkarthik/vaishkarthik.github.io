my writing

three ways of looking at an iphone

a medley of 2 a.m. thoughts

I
no voices, no breaths
only shattered glass and the blood stained floor
paramedics trying to piece together
broken bodies, broken hearts, and missing identities
bullets fired the ring ring ring of loved ones reaching out
but there's no one who can answer.
II
heads down, fingers tapping
feet moving on this ground,
but my head's in another place
typing what i meant to say,
saying what i meant to type
how is it that i am here with you now, but listening to what they have to say?
III
a wall of protection
where we can put ourselves out there,
without really having to be out there.
voices, words and photos i would never show in person,
voices, words and photos that they are not in person.
yet fingers scrolling, we compare,
feeling too skinny, too old, too fond of dessert
delete, and repost
only to realize the filter is a shade too dark.
navigate
home resume projects writing music
